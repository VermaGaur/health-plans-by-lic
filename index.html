About LIC Jeevan Arogya Plan

LIC Jeevan Arogya Plan is a non-linked health insurance plan offered by the Life Insurance Corporation of India. It provides financial protection against the rising cost of medical treatment and hospitalization expenses due to illness or accident. The policy covers hospitalization, surgery, and major medical illnesses.

Features

  • Covers hospitalization expenses, surgery, and major medical illnesses
  • Provides daily hospital cash benefit
  • No pre-policy medical check-up required for a policyholder below 55 years of age
  • Flexible premium payment options
  • Tax benefits under Section 80D of the Income Tax Act, 1961

Benefits

  • Hospitalization expenses covered up to the sum insured
  • Major surgical benefit paid as a lump sum
  • Daycare procedures and treatments covered
  • Daily hospital cash benefit for hospitalization beyond 24 hours
  • Optional critical illness rider available for added protection
  • Tax benefits under Section 80D of the Income Tax Act, 1961

FAQ

What is the minimum age to buy LIC Jeevan Arogya Plan?

The minimum age to buy LIC Jeevan Arogya Plan is 18 years.

What is the maximum age to buy LIC Jeevan Arogya Plan?

The maximum age to buy LIC Jeevan Arogya Plan is 65 years.

What is the policy term for LIC Jeevan Arogya Plan?

The policy term for LIC Jeevan Arogya Plan is 1 year, renewable every year.

What is the premium payment term for LIC Jeevan Arogya Plan?

The premium payment term for LIC Jeevan Arogya Plan is the same as the policy term.
